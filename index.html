+

SparkCrew AI Partners integrate natively through the Bitrix24 Marketplace. Once installed, they appear as team members in your workspace, can participate in conversations, access relevant data, and provide insights directly within your existing workflows.

What makes each AI Partner unique?

+

Each partner has a distinct personality, expertise area, and communication style. Is my business data secure?

+

Absolutely. Your data never leaves your Bitrix24 environment. We use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with SOC 2 and GDPR standards. AI Partners process information within your secure workspace and don't store or share data externally.

Can I switch or add partners later?

+

Yes! You can upgrade your plan to add more partners or switch between partners at any time. Your business context and history are preserved, so new partners can immediately contribute based on your existing operations.

What happens during the early access period?

+

Early access members get priority onboarding, special launch pricing, direct feedback channels with our team, and first access to new features. We'll work closely with you to ensure SparkCrew delivers maximum value for your specific business needs.

Do I need technical knowledge to use SparkCrew?

+

Not at all. SparkCrew is designed for business users, not developers. Installation is one-click through the Bitrix24 Marketplace, and your AI Partners communicate in plain business language. If you can use Bitrix24, you can use SparkCrew.
